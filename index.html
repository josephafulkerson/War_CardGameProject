Welcome to the card game 'WAR'

The goal is to score more points than your opponent. The dealer! At the start, 2 cards will be drawn every round. After the reveal the player with the highest value card will win that round and be awarded one point. After 52 cards have been drawn the game will end. The player with the most points at the end of every round will be the winner. Instructions: Click on the Name to submit a name for your character. Click on the deck in the top left corner in order to "draw" 2 cards. Click on your card to reveal its suit and number. Click on the card again after the point total is tallied to discard the card. To continue, click on the deck once more until all 52 cards have been used. Ready, Set, WAR!
