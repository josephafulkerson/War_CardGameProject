Welcome to the card game 'WAR'

The goal is draw a higher card than the bot. 2 cards will be drawn every round, and the highest value card will win that round and be awarded one point. After 52 cards have been drawn the game will end. The player with the most points at the end will be the winner. Instructions: Click on the Name to submit a name for your character. Click on the deck in the top left corner to draw. Keep an eye on your score to make sure the bot is playing fair! Ready, Set, WAR!
